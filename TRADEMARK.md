# Trademark & Naming Policy

“LSAS”, “Layered Safety and Accuracy System”, and “LSAS Framework” are names associated with this project and are used to identify the specification and related materials authored by Matt Vegas.

## Allowed use (nominative use)
You may use “LSAS” or “LSAS Framework” to truthfully refer to this project, for example:
- “Compatible with LSAS”
- “Implements the LSAS specification”
- “Based on LSAS (see DOI/repo)”

## Not allowed use (branding)
You may not use “LSAS” or “LSAS Framework” as the name of your product, service, or company, or in a way that implies endorsement or official affiliation, without written permission.

Examples of prohibited use without permission:
- Naming a commercial product “LSAS Framework”
- Marketing a proprietary offering as “the LSAS Framework” without attribution and without clearly distinguishing it from this project

## Why this exists
The Apache 2.0 License governs code/content reuse. This naming policy exists to reduce confusion and preserve clear attribution of origin.

If you want permission for branding use, contact the maintainer via the repository contact links.
